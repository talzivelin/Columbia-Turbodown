  • Day 1 - Bienvenido Chile

    Touchdown in Punta Arenas on LAN Airlines, and hop on a shuttle to EcoCamp Patagonia in the heart of Torres del Paine.

  • Day 2 - Journey to the Center of the Paine Massif

    Board a catamaran on Lake Pehoe and sail into the heart of the Paine Massif, where a challenging hike rewards you with stunning views of famous peaks.

  • Day 3 - Glacier Grey

    Hop aboard the catamaran once more, this time to explore Lake Grey and witness glaciers calving from the blue face of Glacier Grey.

  • Day 4 - The Three Towers

    Scramble up a steep boulder field to experience a once-in-a-lifetime view of the famous Torres del Paine, three granite monoliths towering over a glacial lake.

  • Day 5 - Adios, Torres del Paine

    Take a road trip from Torres del Paine to Ushuaia where your cruise ship will be waiting.

See Contest Rules

  • Day 6 - The City in The South

    Spend a day in Ushuaia before boarding a Quark Expeditions ship.

  • Day 7 - Climb Aboard

    Sail down the historic Beagle Channel, transecting the Tierra del Fuego archipelago.

  • Day 8 - Wave Goodbye to Chile

    Get to know Quark’s Expedition Team and learn about what lies ahead on your expedition to the last untouched place on earth.

  • Day 9 - Crossing the Drake

    Before you explore Antarctica, you first must cross the infamous Drake Passage. While the waters can be rough, the onboard living is easy.

  • Days 10 to 14 - Welcome to the 7th Continent

    Hop aboard Zodiac boats and cruise around giant icebergs in uncharted bays and channels. Explore landing sites and hike the unique terrain of the Antarctica Peninsula, meeting curious penguins along the way.

  • Days 15 to 16 - Drake Redux

    Your return across the Drake offers one more chance to enjoy the sea birds and crisp Antarctic air before arriving back to civilization.

  • Day 17 - Homeward Bound

    Land once more in Ushuaia to catch your flight home.
